Biochemical Differentiation

Catalase Test

Differentiates Staphylococcus (+) from Streptococcus (-). Tests for the enzyme that breaks down $H_2O_2$.

Oxidase Test

Identifies bacteria with Cytochrome c oxidase. Differentiates Pseudomonas (+) from Enterobacteriaceae (-).

Coagulase Test

Differentiates S. aureus (+) from other Staphylococci. Tests for fibrin clot formation.

Differentiation Summary

"The classification of bacteria has moved from strictly phenotypic (how they look/react) to genotypic (their genetic sequence). 16S rRNA sequencing is the modern gold standard for defining species."
